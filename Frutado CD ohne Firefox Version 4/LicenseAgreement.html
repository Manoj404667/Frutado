This is the beta version of the Frutado learning units. This version contains the modules Demand Planning (DP), Supply Network Planning (SNP), Production Planning / Detailed Scheduling (PP/DS) and Global Available to Promise (ATP). These modules have been recorded at different points in time.

Users may find it difficult to get insights into the structure of the learning units and the concepts underlying the hierarchical planning system designed for the Frutado AG. Hence, the authors currently are preparing a textbook addressing the Frutado case and its implementation with SAP SCM (APO).
