'Detailed Scheduling Planning Board'

Construction of the Detailed Scheduling Planning Board

The Detailed Scheduling Planning Board is used for the graphical representation of the planning situation with a focus on the different aspects of scheduling. For example, it is possible to show the resource loading situation or pegging relationships between orders and receipts over time.

Various scheduling functions and heuristics are available. Furthermore, the Detailed Scheduling Planning Board is used for the interactive solution of planning problems. The interactive scheduling in the Detailed Scheduling Planning Board can be used to treat planning problems not solved by the heuristics or the optimizer manually and to use expert knowledge about details not maintained in the system .
