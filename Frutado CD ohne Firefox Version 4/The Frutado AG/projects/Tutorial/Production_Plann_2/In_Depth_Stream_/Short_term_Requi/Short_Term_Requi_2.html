Short term Requirement

In this scenario we want to aquire a new customer who has an urgent demand for a fresh supply of beverages. Because no receipts are available and the safety stock is not large enough, new short term orders have to be planned.

As the production line can be overloaded by the additional requirements, we need to prioritize the production orders which are linked with the short term requirements over production orders that are based only on forecasts. This way we can make sure that the customer demand will be covered in time.
