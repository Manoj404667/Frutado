Execute HEU_PLAN_STANDARDLOTS

You can use this heuristic, which is based on the algorithm HEU_PLAN_STANDARDLOTS, to execute procurement planning to cover product requirements. During procurement planning, the heuristic is able to create new receipts. To determine the procurement quantities, the heuristic uses the standard lot-sizing procedure and the lot size settings from the location product master or from the heuristic settings.

Procedure

1.  The heuristic updates the pegging relationships and executes a net requirements calculation.

The heuristic does not consider not transfered SNP receipts* in the PP/DS horizon during the net requirements calculation; instead it deletes them. Transfered SNP receipts, on the other hand, are retained and are taken into consideration during the net requirements calculation.

2.  The heuristic executes a procurement quantity calculation to create receipts for the uncovered requirements identified by the net requirements calculation.

3.  If the receipt quantities that the heuristic calculates for an uncovered requirement exceed this requirement (e.g. because of fixed lot sizes), the heuristic pegs this surplus to the following requirement.

4.  The heuristic executes a source determination (selecting a PPM and the mode) based on the procurement quantities and the desired availability dates.

5.  The heuristic plans each newly created order on the resources and pegs it to receipts.

*: compare the SNP-lesson "Conversion of SNP2PPDS"
