Result of the automatic Planning

To see the result of the automatic planning run, the Product Planning Table can be used with its different views of the planning situation. With these views you can evaluate the requirement and procurement situation of  products as well as the resource utilization and allocation. In case of an overloaded resource you can determine the causing requirements and reschedule those orders.

In this lesson, we will check the requirements and the resource allocation. Furthermore we will check if the resources are overloaded and how many requirements are covered by a single order due to the lot size of maximum 4 hl which affects the pegging structure.
