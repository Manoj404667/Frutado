Releasing Customer Demands

In the Frutado project, at the beginning of each week, customer orders are known to the system for the length of one week, and after that we have demand forecasts at DC level. Since, there is no ERP to release feed our system with customer orders, we uploaded customer orders in the system livecatche in the demand planning (DP) module. In this lesson, we show how to consider the uploaded customer orders as customer demand forecasts and release them into our deployment planning version.
