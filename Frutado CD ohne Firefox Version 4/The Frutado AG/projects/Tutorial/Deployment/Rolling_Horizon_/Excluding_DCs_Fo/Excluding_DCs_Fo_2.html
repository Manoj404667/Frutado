Excluding Demand Forecasts at DCs and Transport Orders 

In the Frutado project, at the beginning of each week, customer orders are known to the system for the length of one week, and after that we have demand forecasts at DC level.

Therefore, instead of running deployment only on the basis of DC's forecasts, we consider the actual customer orders for the first week to generate a more appropriate deployment plan. Moreover, to generate a more flexible space for deployment planning, we delete the planned stock transports that are generated by the previous planning modules.

In this lesson, we show how to delete these figures.
