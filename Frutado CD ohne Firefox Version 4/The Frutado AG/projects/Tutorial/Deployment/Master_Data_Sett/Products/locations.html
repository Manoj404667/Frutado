Maintaining products for deployment

To run the deployment planning modules the product related penalties should be maintained in master data. These penalties include the no-delivery and late delivery costs at DC and customer locations. Further, the maximum acceptable delay, for late delivered products are maintained here.

This lesson also displays how to set the deployment planning horizons for individual products, how to use the available demand or supply patterns for deployment planning, and how to define priorities for certain products. As customers planning was not considered before this stage, we also define storage costs at customer locations.
