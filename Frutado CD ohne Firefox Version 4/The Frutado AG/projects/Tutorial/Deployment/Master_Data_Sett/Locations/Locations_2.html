'Locations'

Maintaining Locations for Deployment

Locations are required for procurement, production, and storage of products or components. This short lesson shows where you can maintain the deployment related settings of locations for the Frutado supply chain. You learn how to create category groups to represent the demand and supply figures that should be used for deplyoment planning, and how to assign them to the locations.

In the Frutado project, two classes of demand, customers sales orders/forecasts and DCs forecasts, are considered. The supply figure stems from production quantities produced in plants.
