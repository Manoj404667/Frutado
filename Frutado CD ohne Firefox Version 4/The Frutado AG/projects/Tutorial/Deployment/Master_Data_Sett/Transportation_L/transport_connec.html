Maintaining Transportation lanes for deployment

Transportation lanes serve as the connections between different locations of the Frutado supply chain. Thus, they are crucial for mapping the material flows.

Although transportation lanes are defined in the master data section of SNP, this lesson briefly look at some of the deployment related features of transportation lanes.
