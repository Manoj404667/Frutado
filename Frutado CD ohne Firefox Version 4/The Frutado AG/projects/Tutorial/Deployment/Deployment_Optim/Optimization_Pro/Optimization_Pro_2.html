Deployment Optimization Profile 

The deployment optimization profile is used to make deployment optimization settings. Many of the settings can be replaced by the respective products master data, explained in the Master data settings section. While defining deployment related settings in product master data provides more flexibility in treating products and locations in different ways, the defined settings in deployment optimization profile are equally appllied to all locations and products. 

Besides SNP optimization and cost profiles, the following profiles might also be employed for deployment optimization which are out of the scope of the Frutado AG:

  • SNP lot size profile
  • SNP optimization bound profile
  • SNP planning profile
  • Parallel processing profile

In this lesson we show how to create/maintain a deployment optimization profile and explain its different settings. More specifically, we discuss the following topics:

  • Deployment solution methods
  • Deployment strategies
  • Priority vs. cost-based deployment
  • Constraints consideration
  • Demand priority settings

At the end the influence of setting different deployment strategies under inventory shortage situation is illustrated.
