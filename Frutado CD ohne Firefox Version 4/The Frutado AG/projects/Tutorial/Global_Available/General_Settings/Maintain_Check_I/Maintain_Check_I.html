Maintain Check Instructions

In this section you check whether the necessary check instructions are defined. The check instructions determine type and scope of availability checks.

As precondition you already maintained a business event and a check mode. In fact the check instructions are determined from the combination of check mode and business event. You can have a look at the valid combinations of check modes and business events in the check instructions.

The following settings that you determine in the check instructions are relevant for the Frutado case:

  • basic methods to be executed
  • the sequence of the executed basic methods
  • use of rules-based availability check
  • immediate start of rules-based ATP
  • calling production
