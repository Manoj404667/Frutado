Maintain Business Event

The business event describes an action within a business and its environment. It initiates the product availability check.

Usually the business event is transferred from a connected Online-Transaction-Processing-System (OLTP-system) via CIF integration model. Together with the check mode the business event determines the check instructions.

In this activity you check whether all relevant business events are created.
