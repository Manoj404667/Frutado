Maintain Category

In this section you can look at the pre-defined categories of SAP APO. The categories are part of the settings for the scope of check that is used for the product availability check. They are also important for rules-based availability checks and product allocations.

Note: In this step you should also check the Product View of PP/DS in order to see the category types of the elements that are listed there.
