Maintain Check Control and Scope of Check

In this section you define check control and scope of check. To maintain check control and scope of check an ATP group and a business event already need to be defined.

In fact the check control is determined from the combination of ATP group and business event. You can have a look at the valid combinations of ATP groups and business events in the check control.

The scope of check is part of the check control and contains the selected ATP categories.
