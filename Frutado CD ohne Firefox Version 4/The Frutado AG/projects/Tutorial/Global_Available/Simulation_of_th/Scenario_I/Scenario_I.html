Scenario I

In the first scenario the following situation is illustrated:

  • customer order arrives at the system
  • customer: FRU_KUNDE_01 (appropriate distribution center is: FRU_DC_01)
  • requested product: FRU_SAFT_01
  • check mode: 030
  • business event: A
  • desired quantity: 2 HL
  • requested date: 20.04.2009

The data of this customer request are chosen arbitrarily for this example.

In this lession you will learn how to simulate a product availbility check. Moreover you learn how to interpret the result screen of the product availability check.
