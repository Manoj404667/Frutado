Scenario III

In the third scenario the following situation is built:

  • customer order arrives at the system
  • customer: FRU_KUNDE_01 (appropriate distribution center is: FRU_DC_01)
  • requested product: FRU_SAFT_01
  • check mode: 050
  • business event: A
  • desired quantity: 10 HL
  • requested date: 20.04.2009

In scenario II you already carried out a simulative product availability check where the confirmed quantity of the customer`s requested product in the requested location was not large enough. In a further step you also have the possibility to use substitution options, for example check alternative locations for available products, in order to find the remaining open quantity.

In this lession you will learn how to simulate a rules-based availability check. Moreover you learn how to interpret the result screen of the rules-based  availability check.
