'Conversion SNP2PPDS'

Conversion of SNP orders to PP/DS

The PP/DS plans based on the SNP decisions about sources of the supply, inventory at the end of the buckets and overtime capacity. Hence, the corresponding data has to be transferred to the PP/DS. The management decides to use the APO tool "Conversion SNP2PPDS" to provide the SNP results for the PP/DS module. In this lesson you learn how use this tool.

Note that you have to consider the details given in lesson "Integration of SNP and PP/DS" if you use the conversion tool.
