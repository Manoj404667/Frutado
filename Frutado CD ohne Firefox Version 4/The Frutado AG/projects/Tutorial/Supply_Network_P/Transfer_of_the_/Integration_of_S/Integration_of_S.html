Integration of SNP and PP/DS

In order to transfer the SNP results to the PP/DS module, it is necessary to understand the links between these two modules and how to define them. These links are for example the planning horizon and the master data. Therefore it is helpful if you have finished the lessons "Maintaining Resources for SNP" and "Maintaining PPMs for SNP" before you continue with this lesson.
