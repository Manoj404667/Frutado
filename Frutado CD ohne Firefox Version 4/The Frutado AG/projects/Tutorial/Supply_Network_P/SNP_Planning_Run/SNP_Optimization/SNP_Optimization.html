'SNP Optimization'

The SNP Optimization Process

The Frutado management wants to use the cost-based optimizer to create a minimum cost master plan for the overall Frutado supply chain. The optimizer determines the optimal sources of supply, the inventory at the end of the buckets, and available overtime capacity to fulfill the forecasted weekly demand provided by the demand planning module.

In order to understand how Frutado constraints and requirements are implemented, it is helpful to know the lessons "SNP Master Data" and "Model Building" as well as the lesson "SNP Planning Book". 

To analyze the planning results on your own, an excel file is provided additionally. The export of such a file is a standard fuction of SAP APO.
