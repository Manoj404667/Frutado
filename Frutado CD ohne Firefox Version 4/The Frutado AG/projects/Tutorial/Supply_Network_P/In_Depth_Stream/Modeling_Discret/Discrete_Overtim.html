'Discrete Overtime'

Modelling Discrete Capacity Extensions in SNP

The Frutado AG produces 24 hours on 5 days a week as standard. To increase production capacity up to 24 hours overtime can be used on Saturdays. However, it seems economically reasonable to the management that if overtime is worked on Saturday, the complete 24 hours have to be scheduled. This is implemented by modelling discrete capacity extensions.
