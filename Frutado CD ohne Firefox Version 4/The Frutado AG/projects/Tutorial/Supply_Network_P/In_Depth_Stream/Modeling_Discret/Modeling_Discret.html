'Modeling Discrete Overtime'

Modeling Discrete Capacity Extensions in SNP

Without using overtime, the Frutado AG produces 24 hours on 5 days a week. In order to increase production capacity up to 24 hours, overtime can be used on Saturdays. However, it seems economically reasonable to the management that if overtime is worked on Saturday, the complete 24 hours have to be scheduled. This is implemented by modeling discrete capacity extensions.

Again, an excel file is provided to analyze the results on your own.
