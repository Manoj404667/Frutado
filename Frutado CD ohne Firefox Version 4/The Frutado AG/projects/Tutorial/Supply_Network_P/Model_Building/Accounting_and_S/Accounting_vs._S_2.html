Accounting vs. Steering Costs

In this lesson the cost model for the Frutado AG is presented. By implementing the SNP cost structure you have to distinguish between real and steering costs. Steering costs are used to penalize the violation of soft constraints, for example they can be used to influence inventory targets or the customer service level. 

As already known, the majority of costs can be maintained in the master record. In this lesson another option the "Maintain Cost Directory" is presented. If you want to create a cost function, you also need these transactions.
