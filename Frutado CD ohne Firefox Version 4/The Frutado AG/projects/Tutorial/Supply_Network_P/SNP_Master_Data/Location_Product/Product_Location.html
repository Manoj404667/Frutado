'Location Product'

Maintain The Location Products for SNP

With location products are products meant, whose parameters are dependent of the Lokations they are related to. Thus, you can easily imagine that storage costs for a product not only depend on the characteristics of the product, but also on the place where they are stored. And this is also true for some other attributes of the Frutado products. This lesson shows you wich parameters these are and where you can change them.

As adopted the Frutado AG holds all products in all distribution centers. That are 3x19 = 57 location products solely for the possible combinations of distribution centers and products. For the production plants, this is a bit different. Because at an individual production plant only a part of the entire product portfolio is produced. Thus there are only 29 location products related to the production plants. For further informations about the production possibilities please read the general information about the Frutado AG.
