'Location Product'

Maintaining Location Products for SNP

Location products are products, whose parameters are dependent on the location they are related to. For example storage costs of a product do not only depend on the characteristics of the product, but also on the place where it is stored. And this holds for some other attributes of the Frutado products, too.
This lesson displays the relevant parameters and where to edit them.

The Frutado AG holds the complete product range (19 products) in all three distribution centers. Consequently, there are 3 x 19 = 57 location products solely for the possible combinations of distribution centers and products. At an individual production plant, only a part of the entire product portfolio can be produced. Hence, there are just 29 location products related to the production plants. For further information about the production possibilities, please read the general information about the Frutado AG.
