Maintain The Ressources For SNP

To be able to produce or transport products, various resources are needed. In the SNP, we balance scarce capacity while taking the costs into account. In the case of Frutado AG these are especially the filling lines.

Even we need means of transportation to model the material flow in SAP APO, but in contrast to the filling lines they are not a bottleneck. However the production cost of filling lines are maintained in the PPMs. Thus our focus in this lesson is on the adjustment of available capacity and the extra costs for overtime.
