Maintaining Resources for SNP

Various resources are needed to be able to produce and transport the products. Transportation is performed by a third party logistics and, hence, the corresponding resources do not represent a bottleneck. But the use of the scarce production resources, i. e. the filling lines, has to be balanced.
The resource consumption and according production costs are maintained in the PPMs that are described in the next lesson. Thus, the focus in this lesson is put on the adjustment of available capacity and the definition of overtime.
