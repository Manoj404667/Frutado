'Location'

Maintaining Locations for SNP

Locations are required for procurement, production, and storage of products or components. This short lesson shows where you can maintain the locations of the Frutado supply chain. You learn how to choose a location type, the time zone, and the local calendar.

SAP APO allows for more than ten location types. However, in the Frutado case only distribution centers, production plants, and customers locations are needed. But as each Frutado customer is assigned to exactly one distribution center, the customer locations are not relevant for the SNP model.
