'Production Process Model'

Maintaining Production Process Models
for SNP

Production Process Models are very essential for the SNP. They determine which operations, activities, components, and resources are needed for a specific production process.

In this lesson you get to know the structure of a typical Frutado production process model (PPM) and how to maintain the SNP relevant parameters.
