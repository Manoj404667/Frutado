'Transportation Lane'

Maintaining Transportation Lanes for SNP

Transportation lanes represent the connections between the different locations of the Frutado supply chain. Thus, they are crucial for mapping the material flows.

Since the Frutado AG is cooperating with a third party logistics, only one means of transportation is provided and sufficient capacity is always available. Besides, the duration and costs for the use of a transportation lane are equal for all products because all products are beverages with no product specific requirements.
