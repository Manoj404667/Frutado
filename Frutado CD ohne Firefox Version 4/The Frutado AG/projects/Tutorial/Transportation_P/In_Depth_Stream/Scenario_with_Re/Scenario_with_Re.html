Scenario with Reduced Shift Lenght

In the lesson TP/VS Optimization a planning run was made for the Frutado scenario. The results showed that fixed vehicle cost was the highest cost and that all orders could be delivered with a very small delay cost.

In this lesson we will look into an alternative scenario, where the maximum duration of a trip is set to 8 hours, instead of 10 hours as before.  The reason for this could be that the trade unions have set a new requirement that the drivers are not allowed to work for more than 8 hours. After running the new scenario, the results will be analyzed and compared.
