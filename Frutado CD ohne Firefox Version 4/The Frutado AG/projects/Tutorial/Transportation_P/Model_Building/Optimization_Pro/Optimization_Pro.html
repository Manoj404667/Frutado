Defining an Optimization Profile

In order to define the scope of the TP/VS for different planning scenarios, different optimization profiles can be maintained to restrict the

  • cost profiles 
  • TP/VS horizons
  • types of orders (ATP categories)
  • locations
  • resources

included in a certain TP/VS planning scenario.

In this lesson you will practice defining an optimization profile through maintaining the respective settings for the Frutado project.
