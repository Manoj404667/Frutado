Defining Windows for Pickup/Delivery

Different constraints are taken into account during the optimization. Generally they can be categorized into hard and soft constraints, or a combination of both. While violation of a hard constraint will result in an infeasible solution, soft constraints might be violated subject to some penalty costs.

In this lesson you will learn how to use hard and soft constraints when defining windows for pickup/delivery.

Note that these pickup/delivery windows, as they are defined in SAP APO, are not related to the opening hours of the customer. This needs to be clarified since in the literature the opening hours of customer are usually referred to as time windows.
