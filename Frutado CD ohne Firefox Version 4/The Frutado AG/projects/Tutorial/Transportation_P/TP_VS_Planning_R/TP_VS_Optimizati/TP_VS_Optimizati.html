TP/VS Optimization

In interactive vehicle scheduling orders are assigned to vehicle resources, either manually or by using the optimizer. As a result planned shipments are generated from the optimization data (that is; orders, deliveries and vehicles).

In this lesson you will learn how to run interactive vehicle scheduling by using the TP/VS optimizer. The shipments are planned on the basis of costs and the optimal delivery sequence for the vehicles as well as transportation dates/times is established. Analyzing the results is also of importance to see if the scheduled shipments meet the expectation of the planner.
