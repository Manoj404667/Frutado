Maintaining Vehicle Resources for TP/VS 

There are two types of transport-relevant resources in APO: transportation resources and vehicle resources. Transportation resources are used in aggregated supply network planning and deployment, while vehicle resources are used in detailed transportation planning (TP/VS).

Vehicle resources are used as planning objects in TP/VS, they show the availability of vehicles used for transportation. A vehicle resource is a concrete means of transport by which the transportation between locations is executed. Each vehicle resource must be assigned to one means of transport.

This lesson shows where you can maintain the vehicle resources and its relevant parameters for the Frutado scenario.
