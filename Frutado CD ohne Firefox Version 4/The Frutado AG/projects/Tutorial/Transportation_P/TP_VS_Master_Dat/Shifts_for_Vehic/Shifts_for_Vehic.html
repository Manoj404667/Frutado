Assigning Shifts to Vehicle Resources

In SAP APO it is possible to define daily shifts, for the vehicle resources, for any number of consecutive days (for example a week). Working and break times are defined for each shift, where shift sequences are assigned to time intervals of a capacity variant.

The vehicle resources have already been described in an earlier lesson, however some details were left out. The vehicle resources are weekday-dependent and they are only available for 10 hours on their weekday. Therefore the vehicle resources are actually assigned to shifts to make them only available on their corresponding weekday. In this lesson you will learn how to maintain the shifts for the vehicle resources.
