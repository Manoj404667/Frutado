The Supply Chain Engineer (SCE)

The Supply Chain Engineer is used to create and maintain network models of supply chains. The Supply Chain Engineer screen provides an overview of your model and you can perform the following tasks:

·         Supply chain model maintenance

·         Transportation lane maintenance

·         Execution of queries

 

The purpose of this lesson is to show an overview of the Supply Chain Engineer, because it is a convenient tool for accessing the data of different components of the supply chain, and the TP/VS module accordingly.
