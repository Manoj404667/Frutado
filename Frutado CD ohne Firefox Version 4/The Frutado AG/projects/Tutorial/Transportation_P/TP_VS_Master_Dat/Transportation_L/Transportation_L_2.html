'Transportation Lanes'

 

Maintaining Transportation Lanes for TP/VS 

Transportation lanes represent the connections between different locations of the Frutado supply chain. They can be thought of as a road between two locations.

The system can automatically calculate the transportation duration and distance for each lane. Those are calculated based on geographical coordinates, the multiplication factor and the speed of the means of transport.

This lesson explains how to create a transportation lane between two customer locations and to maintain its relevant settings including:

  • The assignment of means of transport to a lane and their respective usage costs.
  • Restricting the products that can be transported on a lane.
