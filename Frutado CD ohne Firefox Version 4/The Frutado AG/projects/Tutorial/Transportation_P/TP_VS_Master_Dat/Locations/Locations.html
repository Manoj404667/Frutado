Maintaining Locations for TP/VS  

The precise locations of all customers and distribution centers are needed in order to determine their geographical longitude and latitude. This is important for calculation of transportation distances and durations between the locations.

SAP APO allows for more than ten location types, in the Frutado case only distribution centers, production plants and customer locations are needed. However, as the transportation between plant and DC is not considered, the plant locations are not relevant for the TP/VS module.

This lesson shows where you can maintain the locations and the parameters which are of importance for the TP/VS module, such as the address of the customer, their priority and the available loading resources.
