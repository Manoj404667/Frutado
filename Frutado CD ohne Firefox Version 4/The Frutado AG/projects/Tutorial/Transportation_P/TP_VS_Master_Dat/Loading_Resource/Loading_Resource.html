Maintaining Loading Resources for TP/VS 

Loading resources are used for loading and unloading vehicles whilst taking into account their capacity.

In the Frutado project, it is assumed that three loading resources are available at each distribution center, and one loading resource at each customer location to do the unloading task. A loading resource can serve only one vehicle at a time, which means, the other vehicles, when more than one vehicle need to be loaded/unloaded, must wait until a loading resource is free.

The opening hours of distribution centers and customers are modeled through the settings of their loading resources. The loading resources are acting as constraints in the optimization, since their time windows have to be respected and loading/unloading can only take place during the opening hours.

This lesson shows how to maintain the loading resources for the Frutado project.
