'Means of Transport'

Maintaining Means of Transport for TP/VS 

Means of transport refers to the types of vehicles used, e.g. different types of trucks or ships. Three different types of trucks are used in the Frutado project.

Creating means of transport is the initial stage in defining TP/VS master data. The created means of transport are later assigned to transportation lanes and are used to classify vehicle resources in the subsequent lessons, see the figure below.

 

This short lesson shows where you can maintain the means of transport for the Frutado supply chain. You learn how to set the average speed, the multiplication factor for the Euclidean distance, the setup time and the working time for the means of transport.
