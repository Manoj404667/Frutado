Edit the Planning Data for the Promotion 

After you have created a promotion you have to assign objects to it.
There are two ways to assign objects.
With the multiple assignment you can assign multiple objects at once. The promotion level is automatically included. As you are working with a Promotion Base, the characteristics in the Promotion Base are copied to the promotion automatically. You can remove any characteristics but you can only add those characteristics that are contained in the promotion base.
With the single assignment you can assign characteristics together with values at individual characteristic level.
In the following example, use the multiple assignment to assign the aggregated product group "FRU_SEAS1_DP_PROMO".
After you have assigned the object the grid shows the single products of the group.
Now enter the expected influence of the current promotion. It is expected that the discount of 10 0x1.552c00p-990s a demand-increasing-impact of 54573740n the whole aggregated group during the 8 weeks.
The system calculates the absolute numbers.
To take these expectations into account in Interactive Planning, you have to change the status of the promotion to "Planned in the future".

Now you can check the results in Interactive Planning.
