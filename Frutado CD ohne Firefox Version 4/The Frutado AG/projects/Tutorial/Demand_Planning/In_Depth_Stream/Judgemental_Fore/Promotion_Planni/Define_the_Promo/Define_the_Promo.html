Define the Promotion level for the planning area 

The objective of this lesson is to define the Promotion Level. By defining the Promotion Level you decide, for instance, if you want to plan promotions for products or locations.
The Frutado AG plans to give a 10Discount on the Aggregated Product Group "FRU_SEAS1_DP_PROMO". That means that we have to choose Product as the Promotion Level.
