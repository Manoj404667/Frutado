Create a Promotion 

The objective of this lesson is to create a new Promotion ("Discount for FRU_SEAS1_DP_PROMO"). This Promotion will take place in the beginning of the year 2009 (Start: January 5th, 2009) and will last for eight weeks. It is expected that due to the promotion, the demand for the Aggregated Product Grup FRU_SEAS1_DP_PROMO will increase about 5

You can create new Promotions In the Promotion Planning screen.

As you use a Promotion Base you only has to fill in the name, the description, the type and the period of the promotion.

You create the Promotion by saving these information. The system immediately generates a grid for the promotion. From then on the promotion is available in the system, but not yet active.
