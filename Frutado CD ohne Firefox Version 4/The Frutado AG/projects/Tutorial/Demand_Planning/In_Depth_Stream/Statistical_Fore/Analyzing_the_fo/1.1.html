'Analyzing the forecast results'

After executing a forecast it is useful to analyze the forecasted results and to check if the quality of the forecast is appropriate to the needs of your company.

In SAP APO the "Interactive Demand Planning" desktop is the tool to analyze the forecasts!

The "Interactive Demand Planning Desktop" consists basically of two components.

The selection area, also called "Selector", is the most important tool for searching, sorting and organizing information. You find it on the left side of the "Interactive Demand Planning Desktop".

The second component is the workspace on the right side of the desktop, where you can see the chosen time series.

By clicking the "Univariate Forecast" button, the "Change Mode" is entered. This is the most important desktop to analyze and change the settings and results of the univariate forecast!

In this lesson you learn how you can check the results of the forecasts.
