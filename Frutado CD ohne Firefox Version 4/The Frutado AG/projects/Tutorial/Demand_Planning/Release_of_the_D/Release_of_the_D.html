Release of the Demand Plan to Supply Network Planning 

The values of the demand plan are stored in anonymous liveCache time series objects. When releasing the demand plan these time series objects are transformed into liveCache orders .This leads to the forecasted demand being apparent for the Supply Network Planning, the PP/DS and the Deployment module. All following modules must try to satisfy the forecasted demand by creating feasible plans.

Duration of this Lesson:
Demo Mode: 3min.
Practice Mode: 4min.
