Calculate Proportional Factors 

The supply chain department of the Frutado AG has analyzed the historical data and has come to the conclusion that creating forecasts on the basis of aggregated products would be a good solution. The allocation of products to the aggregated groups takes place in the next chapter “Create Product Aggregates”.

This chapter deals with the calculation of proportional factors which are used when the aggregated groups are split up into their basic form. The base of this calculation is a key figure – in case of the Frutado AG the historical data. The DP-module analyzes the ratio of the accumulated past demand of a product and compares it to the accumulated past demand of the according aggregated group. This method implies that the constitution of the single products in an aggregated group stays the same over the complete period of time.

A time based disaggregation is not needed with the Frutado AG. The demand figures which are needed by the subsequent modules are created in the time granularity needed.
